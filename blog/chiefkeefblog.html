Chief Keef

Dec 11, 2022 by Luis Sanchez

Career

Chief Keef started the drill subgenre movement in hiphop when he was only 16 years old. After his success in his hit record "Love Sosa", a new wave of drill was birthed into hiphop, where rappers who were involved in the streets would rap about their activity in the streets over an aggressive monotone beat

Chief Keef is a father figure in hiphop, as he has made a lot of careers off his sound. His only downfall was not letting go of the streets, where he came form and focusing on his music which was very influential at the time. It was confirmed Apple had their eyes on Chief Keef for a while and wanted to sign him to an exclusive apple deal for millions of dollars but later shelfed that idea when it was clear Chief Keef was not focused on music, but rather gang banging. The deal was later given to #1 hip hop artist in the world, Drake.
