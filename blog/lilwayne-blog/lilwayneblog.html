Lil Wayne

Dec 11, 2022 by Luis

Career

One of the most skilled and influential rappers of our time, Wayne has been in the spotlight since he was 14 years old and since then has been running laps through hiphop culture with his clever metaphors and insane wordplay. Lil Wayne has cemented himself a mogul in hiphop, having influenced a whole generation of rappers, much like Chief Keef.

Aside from his influence, Lil Wayne has also birthed many careers of rappers who are relevant and influential themselves. He's signed notable rappers like Drake, and Nicki Minaj to his label "Young Money". Drake and Nicki Minaj are two prolific figures in hiphop today, currently on a 15+ year run, with the help of Wayne.
