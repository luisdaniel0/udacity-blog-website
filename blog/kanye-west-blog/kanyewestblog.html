Kanye West

Dec 11, 2022 by Luis

Career

Kanye West, One of the most prolific artists in music history, not just hiphop, considered a genius by most music critics is by far the most influential rapper all time. From his iconic production in his early days, or his ten classic albums and their rollouts. Kanye West shifted hiphop from gangster music and elevated it to something more meaningful in terms of quality and production. With many succesful albums under his belt Kanye West is cemented as the greatest of all time.

Kanye West's influence has also transcended music and has reached the fashion world. From his very succesful shoe deal with Adidas, "Yeezys", are Adidas' best selling shoe to this day.
